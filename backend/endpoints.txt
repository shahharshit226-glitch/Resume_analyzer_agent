Use these endpoints:

Business email category data

GET /categorized-mails/dashboard-sections (best for frontend sections/cards)
GET /categorized-mails/all (all business emails)
GET /categorized-mails/{category} (single category like Support, Billing, etc.)
GET /analytics/mail-categories (category counts for charts)
Resume (candidate) data

GET /candidates (all processed resumes with ATS/status)
GET /analytics/summary (totals: candidates, shortlisted, rejected, avg score)
GET /analytics/candidates-per-day
GET /analytics/score-distribution
GET /analytics/top-job-roles
